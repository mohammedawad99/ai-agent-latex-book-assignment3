\chapter{System Design and Architecture}

\section{Overall Pipeline Architecture}

The pipeline is a series of connected stages, each handled by a specialised
agent. It begins with a high-level topic and produces structured content for
later \LaTeX{} assembly. Figure~\ref{fig:architecture} shows the block
diagram of the pipeline.

\begin{figure}[htbp]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.55\textwidth]{figures/architecture.png}
  \caption{Multi-agent pipeline architecture.}
  \label{fig:architecture}
\end{figure}

The topic is first expanded by the planner into a detailed outline. The
writer drafts content from that outline, the reviewer checks the draft for
coverage and consistency, and the reference curator collects citations. The
structured output of the final stage is the input to \LaTeX{} assembly.

\section{Defining the Crew: Agent Roles and Responsibilities}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Planner agent} --- the architect of the structure. Expands
        the topic into a hierarchical outline and defines the sections.
  \item \textbf{Writer agent} --- the author. Drafts the prose for each
        section, aiming for clarity, coherence, and accuracy.
  \item \textbf{Reviewer agent} --- the quality check. Reviews the draft for
        coverage, consistency, and obvious errors, and produces structured
        feedback.
  \item \textbf{Reference curator agent} --- collects and organises the
        citations used in the text.
\end{itemize}

Each role is intentionally narrow so that the responsibility of each step is
clear and the output of each step is easy to inspect.

\section{Task Orchestration and Dependencies}

The crew uses a \texttt{sequential} process. The outline has no upstream
dependency; the draft depends on the outline; the review depends on the
draft; and the references depend on the draft and review. CrewAI manages this
by passing each task's output as context to the next. This sequential flow,
mirroring a normal write-then-review workflow, is what produced the four task
outputs recorded in the Stage 8C.7 run evidence
(\texttt{crew\_outputs/\_index.json}).
